Render generated files/documents inline at [[file:N]] markers (like artifacts)

Extend the artifact-style "marker + inline render" experience to documents and
data files: a [[file:NAME]] marker for a previewable type (pdf/docx/pptx/xlsx/csv/
image/audio/video/html/text) now renders an inline preview card in the reply —
header with filename, expand-to-modal, and download — instead of just a chip.
Unmarked/non-previewable files stay as chips.

FilePreview gains a `compact` prop (bounded heights ~26rem) for inline use;
image -> img, video -> inline player, docx/html/sheet -> shorter iframes/tables,
pdf/pptx -> scrollable. So charts, reports, spreadsheets and slides the model
produces show rendered in-line, consistent with create_artifact.
